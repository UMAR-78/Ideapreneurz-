About us:

Ideapreneurz builds lean, high-impact product engineering and talent teams that turn ideas into results. We focus on aligning teams with business goals and domain expertise, not just tasks.

We solve the common problem of misaligned vendors by delivering purpose-driven teams that move fast and deliver measurable value. Whether launching new products, scaling platforms, or closing talent gaps, we bring clarity, speed, and precision to every engagement.

At Ideapreneurz, the right team structure and sharp focus drive real outcomes.

Wali Khan

Wali Khan

Founder

Wali Khan is a veteran technology leader and founder of Ideapreneurz, built on a clear belief: high-performing engineering teams should think in domains, not just tasks. After two decades of leading product organizations, he consistently encountered the limitations of traditional vendors who prioritized staffing over capability.

Currently the CTO at Statewise (formerly TaskMaster Pro, now part of Cubhub), Wali has built and scaled global teams that deliver innovation, speed, and measurable outcomes. With Ideapreneurz, he brings that same philosophy to clients, helping them form lean, product-driven teams tailored to stage, domain, and scale.

He holds a Master’s in Computer Science and lives in Nashville, TN, with his wife and two daughters.

At Ideapreneurz, she leads the talent function with a clear mission to close hiring gaps with clarity, purpose, and confidence.
